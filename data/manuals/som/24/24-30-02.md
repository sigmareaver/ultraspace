# 24-30-02 — Electrical Power Down

**From:** buses powered. **To:** all sources disconnected.

WARNING: Bus capacitors retain charge after this procedure. Both buses will
indicate near operating voltage with all contactors open. This is normal.
Treat all distribution as energized until verified discharged.

NOTE: Expect BUS E UNDERVOLT and BUS A UNDERVOLT annunciation if loads
remain connected during shutdown; the monitors are armed and live
(24-00-00 §5). This procedure sheds loads first partly to avoid ambiguity
between deliberate shutdown and a distribution casualty.

## Procedure

Execute per checklist `som-24-30-02`:

```
 1. CB A1 (cabin) ............................ OPEN
 2. BUS A tie ................................ OPEN
 3. BUS A precharge .......................... STOP (verify IDLE)
 4. CB E1 (avionics) ......................... OPEN
 5. BAT 1 contactor .......................... OPEN
 6. Battery current .......................... VERIFY < 0.5 A
```
