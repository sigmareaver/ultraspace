# 24-30-04 — Electrical Power Down (UEV Kestrel)

**From:** buses powered per 24-30-03.
**To:** all systems de-energized (cold and dark).

Shed loads before opening sources; open both battery contactors last so
that a stopped shutdown never strands an energized, unmonitored bus.

## Procedure

Execute per checklist `som-24-30-04`:

```
 1. CB A1 (cabin) ............................ OPEN
 2. BUS A tie ................................ OPEN
 3. BUS A precharge .......................... STOP
 4. CB B1 (equipment bay) .................... OPEN
 5. CB E1 (avionics) ......................... OPEN
 6. BAT 2 contactor .......................... OPEN
 7. BAT 1 contactor .......................... OPEN
 8. BAT 1 current ............................ VERIFY < 0.5 A
 9. BAT 2 current ............................ VERIFY < 0.5 A
```

WARNING: Buses retain capacitor charge after this procedure. BUS UNDERVOLT
annunciation during shutdown is normal and confirms the monitors are live
(24-00-00 §5).
